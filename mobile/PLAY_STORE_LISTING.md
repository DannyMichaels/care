## Full Description
Care is your personal wellness companion — a health journal, mood diary, symptom tracker, food log, and medication manager all in one app.

Whether you're managing a chronic condition like IBD, Crohn's, or IBS, building healthier habits, or simply want to be more mindful about your well-being, Care gives you the tools to stay on top of it all.

🩺 MOOD TRACKER & DIARY
Log how you're feeling each day and spot patterns over time. Track your emotional well-being with a simple, intuitive mood journal.

🔍 SYMPTOM TRACKER
Keep a detailed record of what your body is telling you. Log symptoms with severity levels and notes to share with your doctor.

🍽️ FOOD DIARY
Monitor what you eat and how it affects how you feel. Identify food triggers and build a healthier relationship with nutrition.

💊 MEDICATION MANAGER
Stay on top of your prescriptions and daily routines. Never miss a dose with medication tracking and reminders.

✨ DAILY AFFIRMATIONS
Start each day with a positive mindset. Write and revisit personal affirmations to support your mental health.

🤝 COMMUNITY INSIGHTS
Share your wellness journey and connect with others on similar paths. Post insights, learn from the community, and find support.

🌙 DARK MODE
Comfortable viewing day or night with full dark mode support.

WHY CARE?
Care was built by someone with IBD who needed a single app to track everything — mood, symptoms, food, and medications — without juggling multiple tools. If you've ever wished for one place to manage your health, Care is for you.

Unlike other health trackers that focus on just one thing, Care brings it all together so you can see the full picture of your wellness.

Your data stays yours. Care does not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your privacy matters.
